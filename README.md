PRIVACY & DATA OWNERSHIP
FreeFlow is built with an absolute commitment to creative privacy. Your ideas, verses, and vocal takes belong entirely to you. The application architecture enforces this through three core pillars:

1. Local-First & Private iCloud Storage
FreeFlow does not use proprietary third-party servers, databases, or cloud tracking services. All custom imported instrumentals and recorded studio audio files are stored strictly within your application's local sandbox or your private, secure iCloud Documents container. Because data syncing relies entirely on Apple's native iCloud infrastructure, your creative catalog is completely isolated from the developer and outside interception.

2. Dynamic Hardware Lifecycle (Zero Persistent Listening)
Unlike applications that keep hardware streams perpetually open, FreeFlow implements a state-aware, dynamic audio engine graph. The microphone hardware layer is only initialized when you actively tap the record button or explicitly enable the headphones live-monitoring toggle. The exact moment recording is halted and monitoring feedback is disabled, the core audio graph dynamically rebuilds itself to drop all hardware input channel attachments. This completely stops microphone usage and immediately releases the system privacy indicator (such as the macOS menu bar orange dot or iOS privacy light).

3. Zero Analytics Tracking
FreeFlow does not track, log, or profile your vocabulary selections, custom keywords, or writing frequencies. Your workspace is a completely private digital sandbox.
